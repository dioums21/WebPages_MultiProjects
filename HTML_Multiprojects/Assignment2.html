WEBSITES ABOUT PROTECTED AREAS ISSUES

Protected areas are the most effective ways of preserving the nature of our beautiful planet called Earth.
In addition to protecting the wild animals and the ones on the way to extinction, protected areas provide us with many goods
such as food, clean water, medecine and a shield from the impacts of natural disasters.
Unfortunately, today these areas have been subjected to multitude of obsticles that will be presented to you below.
